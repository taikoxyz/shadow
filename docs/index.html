Prove you have ETH.
Without revealing who you are.

Shadow uses ZK proofs on Taiko Network to let you claim ETH without linking your funding address to your recipient. No pool. No mixer. Just math.

$ curl -fsSL "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/taikoxyz/shadow/main/start.sh?v=$(date +%s)" | sh

How it works

01

Create deposit file

Generate a secret and specify up to 5 payment notes. The file lives on your machine and is never uploaded. Max 8 ETH total.

secret: 0x7a2f...e9c1
Note 0: Send 1.0 ETH
02

Derive target address

Shadow deterministically derives a unique address from your secret via SHA256. No private key exists — it's a math commitment.

secret + notes
↓ SHA256
Target: 0x1234...abcd
03

Fund the address

Send ETH to the target address. It is a plain ETH transfer, indistinguishable from any other transaction on-chain.

From: 0xYour...addr
To: 0x1234...abcd
+ 3.5 ETH
04

Generate a ZK proof

RISC Zero runs inside your Docker container and proves the address was funded — without revealing your secret identity.

Prover: RISC Zero
✓ Proof valid
05

Claim your ETH on Taiko

The Shadow contract on Taiko Network verifies the proof, checks the nullifier to prevent double-claims, and mints ETH directly to your recipient addresses. A 0.1% fee applies.

Contract: Verified
Recipient: 0xEFGH...
+ 0.4995 ETH
(0.5 ETH - 0.1% fee)
