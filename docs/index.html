Shadow GitHub ↗

Zero-knowledge · Taiko Network · Open source

Prove you have ETH.
Without revealing who you are.

Shadow uses ZK proofs on Taiko Network to let you claim ETH without linking your funding address to your recipient. No pool. No mixer. Just math.

How it works

01

Create your deposit file

Generate a secret and specify up to 5 payment notes — who receives what amount. The file lives on your machine and is never uploaded. Max 8 ETH total across all notes.

02

Derive a target address

Shadow deterministically derives a unique Ethereum address from your secret and notes via SHA256. No private key exists for this address — it is a mathematical commitment, not a wallet.

03

Fund the address with ETH

Send ETH to the target address from any wallet. It is a plain ETH transfer, indistinguishable from any other on-chain transaction. Nothing marks it as privacy-related.

04

Generate a ZK proof

Click Generate Proof in the web UI. RISC Zero runs inside your Docker container and proves the address was funded — without revealing your secret or linking to your identity. Real-time progress shown via WebSocket. Takes a few minutes.

05

Claim your ETH on Taiko Network

The Shadow contract on Taiko Network verifies the proof, checks the nullifier to prevent double-claims, and mints ETH directly to your specified recipient addresses. A 0.1% fee applies.
