## Call Data vs Derived Inputs

- Claim calldata `IShadow.PublicInput` is `(blockNumber, chainId, amount, recipient, nullifier)`.
- `stateRoot` is **not** caller-provided calldata; it is derived on-chain from `ICheckpointStore` using `blockNumber` and is part of the circuit public inputs and journal binding.
- `noteIndex` and PoW (`powDigest`) are private witness values enforced inside the zkVM guest and are not committed in the public journal.
